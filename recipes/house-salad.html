House Salad

Description

This is a simple salad, courtesy of Barbonzai Lebanese Eatery. A refreshing salad with simple ingredients like cucumbers, tomatoes and romaine lettuce The dressing is a lemon olive oil dressing and is very popular!

Check them out! Click here to view website

Ingredients needed:

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Persian cucumbers
  • Roma tomatoes
  • More secret spices

Instructions:

  1. Mix olive oil and lemon juice in a small bowl at a 3:2 ratio
  2. Add the secret spices
  3. Chop the lettuce
  4. Dice tomatoes and cucumbers
  5. Add together in a large mixing bowl and add dressing to taste!
