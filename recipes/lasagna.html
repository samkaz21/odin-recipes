Lasagna

Description

This is a homemade lasagna with 6 layers of meat, cheese and pasta. High calorie and not healthy! Enjoy Pairs well with some wine, but what doesn't?

Ingredients that will be required for this:

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • More Cheese
  • More Ground Beef
  • Cute basil leaf for garnish
  • Bottle of wine
  • Cooking mood

Steps to creating the perfect lasagna, all steps are important!

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Take a nice gulp of your wine (preferably red)
  3. Start by putting the first layer of pasta on the pyrex dish
  4. Add cheese to cover the whole thing
  5. Add beef in the same manner
  6. Another pasta sheet followed by another layer of cheese and beef
  7. Do X (this means as many as you want) times until your tower of lasagna is complete
  8. Place sheet into oven, if it still fits, and cook for 45 minutes
  9. Open another bottle of Red and take the lasagna out, Careful of burning yourself!!

Enjoy your feast!
